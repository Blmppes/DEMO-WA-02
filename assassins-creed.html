Assassin's Creed

Preview

Assassin's Creed is a series of game that was developed by Ubisoft. It was originated from a story with the same name.


Assassin's Creed is an action-adventure, stealth video game franchise, created by Patrice Désilets, Jade Raymond and Corey May. Developed and published by Ubisoft.
The game is about a war between the order of the Assassins and the Templars. They both fight for peace, the Assassins fight for peace in freedom but the Templars want peace but under their control. IN this game, take place in characters in Assassin team and the last target is to safe the world from the bane of "The First Civillization" but we have to unlock the memories to know what to do so the Templars are also killed - in our player's memories.

Premise

The Assassin's Creed games primarily revolve around the rivalry between two ancient secret societies — the Assassins and the Templar — and their indirect relation to an ancient species pre-dating humanity, referred to within the games as The First Civillization, whose society, along with much of Earth's biosphere, was destroyed by a massive solar storm thousands of years before the games. The games' real-world chronological setting begins in the year 2012, but the gameplay was mostly played in historical settings.
